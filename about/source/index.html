Selected Research Work

High Practical Reinforcement Learning

The application of multi-agent deep reinforcement learning (MADRL) in the real world is hampered by the low sample efficiency of the models and the high cost to collect data. To improve the practicability, we proposes PEDMA, a flexible plugin unit to improve the sample efficiency in MADRL. It consists of three techniques: (i)Parallel Environments (PE), to accelerate the data acquisition; (ii)Experience Augmentation (EA), a novel data augmentation method that utilizes the permutation invariance property of the multi-agent system to reduce the cost of acquiring data; and (iii)Delayed Updated Policies (DUP), to improve the data utilization efficiency of the MADRL model. This work is published in Applied Intelligence, 2021.

Recently, we proposed Deep Recurrent Graph Network (DRGN) for multi-UAV navigation in the mobile base stations task, which is submited to IEEE transactions on Mobile Computing, 2021. As for heterogeneous environments, we proposed Soft Hierarchical Graph Recurrent Networks (Soft-HGRNs) for large-scale partially observable multi-agent tasks, which is submitted in Applied Intelligence, 2021.

Syntax-aware Text-to-Speech

Recently, I’m interested in exploiting syntactic information to improve the prosody in Non-Autoregressive Text-to-Speech (NAR-TTS) modules. A syntax-aware NAR-TTS model named SyntaSpeech is proposed, and the paper is submitted to IJCAI 2022.
