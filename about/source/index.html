Featured Research Work

3D Talking Face Synthesis

I’m currently working on this topic, and the target conference is NeurIPS 2022!

Syntax-aware Text-to-Speech

To exploit syntactic information to improve the prosody in Non-Autoregressive Text-to-Speech (NAR-TTS) modules, a syntax-aware NAR-TTS model named SyntaSpeech is proposed, which is accepted by IJCAI-ECAI 2022.

Deep Reinforcement Learning

Reducing the cost to collect data. The application of multi-agent deep reinforcement learning (MADRL) in the real world is hampered by the low sample efficiency of the models and the high cost to collect data. To improve the practicability, we propose Experience Augmentation (EA),) a novel data augmentation method that utilizes the permutation invariance property of the multi-agent system. This paper is published in Applied Intelligence, 2021.

Graph Reinforcement Lerarning for real-world application. We propose Soft Deep Recurrent Graph Network (SDRGN) to controll the multi-UAV navigation in the mobile base stations task with graph reinforcement learning. This paper is published in IEEE transactions on Mobile Computing, 2021.

More generalized RL for large-scale and heterogeneous problems. As for complicated environments that have hundreds of heterogeneous agents, we proposed Soft Hierarchical Graph Recurrent Networks (Soft-HGRNs) .
